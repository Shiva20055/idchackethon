Our Journey & Milestones

üìÖ 1995 - Foundation Year

HealthCare Plus Hospital was founded with a vision to provide accessible and affordable healthcare to the community. We started with 50 beds and a dedicated team of 25 medical professionals.

üìÖ 2000 - Major Expansion

We expanded our facilities and introduced cardiology and neurology departments. Our bed capacity increased to 200, and we established ourselves as a multi-specialty hospital.

üìÖ 2008 - Technology Integration

We adopted cutting-edge medical technology including CT scanners, MRI machines, and digital systems. This marked our transformation into a modern healthcare facility.

üìÖ 2015 - National Recognition

HealthCare Plus Hospital received national accreditation from the National Board of Accreditation. We were recognized for our clinical excellence and patient care standards.

üìÖ 2020 - Digital Healthcare Launch

We launched our telemedicine services and online appointment booking system during the pandemic, ensuring uninterrupted healthcare delivery to our patients.

üìÖ 2026 - Present Day

Today, we operate 12 specialized departments with over 500 expert doctors and staff. We continue to innovate and provide exceptional healthcare services to thousands of satisfied patients.
